Brief History behind Web Development


                 The Front end development is a balance of programming and layouts that empowers the visuals and user interactions
    of the web product(site or app). In most simple terms, if a web design depicts how the website looks, the front-end development
     is what implements that design on the website. For instance, consider your website as a car. Now the features that make your car
    run (the engine, transmission, exhaust, etc.) will be the backend. On the other hand, the features that make your car look cool like
    a racing stripe, sleek leather interior, etc. are the Front end of the car

All the elements which you can directly interact (Touch, See or Hear)
     with on the web are Front end elements.
